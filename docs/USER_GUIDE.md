## 🚀 Getting Started

### Creating Your First Campaign

1. **Navigate to Campaigns**
   - Click on "Campañas" in the main menu
   - Click the "Nueva Campaña" button

2. **Fill Basic Information** (Step 1)
   - Campaign Name: Descriptive name for your campaign
   - Advertiser: Select or enter advertiser name
   - Agency: Select the advertising agency (if applicable)
   - Campaign Type: Choose from Standard, Sponsorship, or Promotion

3. **Set Financial Details** (Step 2)
   - Net Value: Total campaign budget
   - Agency Commission: Percentage (typically 10-20%)
   - The system will automatically calculate gross value

4. **Configure Dates** (Step 3)
   - Start Date: When the campaign begins
   - End Date: When the campaign ends
   - The system will show duration in days

5. **Select Broadcasters** (Step 4)
   - Choose one or more radio stations
   - View coverage and audience data
   - AI will suggest optimal broadcaster mix

6. **Define Campaign Lines** (Step 5)
   - Specify number of spots per day
   - Set spot duration (15s, 20s, 30s, 60s)
   - Choose time blocks (AM, PM, Prime, Night)

7. **Program Spots** (Step 6)
   - Use automatic ML optimization (recommended)
   - Or manually assign spots to blocks
   - View real-time conflict detection

8. **Review and Confirm** (Step 7)
   - Review all campaign details
   - Check AI validation results
   - Submit for approval

## 🤖 AI-Powered Features

### Automatic Validation

The system continuously validates your campaign data in real-time:

**What it checks**:
- ✅ Required fields completion
- ✅ Budget consistency
- ✅ Date logic
- ✅ Spot availability
- ✅ Commercial conflicts

**How to use**:
1. Simply fill in the form
2. Watch for validation messages on the right panel
3. Green checkmarks = all good
4. Yellow warnings = review recommended
5. Red errors = must fix before proceeding

**AI Auto-Correction**:
- The system can automatically fix common errors
- Look for the "⚡ Apply" button next to suggestions
- Click to accept AI corrections

### Smart Property Suggestions

The AI analyzes your campaign and suggests relevant properties:

**Sector-Specific Suggestions**:
- **Financial**: Legal approvals, regulatory codes
- **Retail**: Seasonal campaigns, product categories
- **Automotive**: Vehicle models, specifications
- **Health**: ISP approvals, health warnings

**How to use**:
1. Fill in advertiser name and campaign details
2. Click "🤖 Get AI Suggestions" in Properties panel
3. Review suggested properties
4. Click "Add" to include them in your campaign

### ML Optimization

Let the genetic algorithm find the optimal spot distribution:

**When to use**:
- Campaigns with 100+ spots
- Complex multi-broadcaster campaigns
- When you want maximum efficiency

**How to use**:
1. Go to Programming tab
2. Click "🧬 Optimize with AI"
3. Select optimization goal:
   - **Maximum Reach**: Prioritize unique audience
   - **Maximum Frequency**: Prioritize repetition
   - **Balanced**: Best of both worlds
4. Review the optimized distribution
5. Click "Apply Optimization"

**What it optimizes**:
- Spot distribution across time blocks
- Broadcaster mix for maximum reach
- Cost efficiency
- Conflict avoidance

## 📊 Understanding Visualizations

### Timeline View

Shows your campaign schedule over time:

- **Green markers**: Campaign start/end
- **Blue markers**: Individual spots
- **Purple markers**: Automatic optimizations
- **Red markers**: Potential conflicts

**How to use**:
- Hover over markers for details
- Click to zoom into specific dates
- Use filters to show/hide marker types

### Heatmap View

Shows spot saturation by day and hour:

- **Green cells**: Low saturation (< 40%)
- **Yellow cells**: Medium saturation (40-70%)
- **Red cells**: High saturation (> 70%)

**How to use**:
- Identify oversaturated time slots
- Find available slots (green areas)
- Balance your distribution

### Performance Dashboard

Real-time campaign performance metrics:

- **Estimated Reach**: Unique audience size
- **Average Frequency**: Repetition rate
- **CPM**: Cost per thousand impressions
- **ROI**: Return on investment estimate
- **Confidence**: AI prediction confidence (0-100%)

## ⚠️ Anomaly Alerts

The AI continuously monitors for unusual patterns:

### Types of Anomalies

**Value Anomalies**:
- Unusually high or low campaign values
- Inconsistent pricing
- Suspicious round numbers

**Behavioral Anomalies**:
- Activity outside normal hours
- Mass creation of campaigns
- Drastic changes in patterns

**Fraud Detection**:
- Potential data manipulation
- Inconsistent related data
- Suspicious patterns

### How to Respond

When you see an anomaly alert:

1. **Review the Details**
   - Read the description carefully
   - Check the detected vs. expected values
   - Note the confidence level

2. **Assess Severity**
   - 🟢 Low: Review when convenient
   - 🟡 Medium: Review soon
   - 🟠 High: Review immediately
   - 🔴 Critical: Stop and verify now

3. **Take Action**
   - Follow the recommended action
   - Verify data accuracy
   - Contact supervisor if needed
   - Document any corrections

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Issue**: "Campaign number already exists"
**Solution**: The system auto-generates unique numbers. If you see this, refresh the page and try again.

**Issue**: "Validation errors won't clear"
**Solution**: 
1. Check all required fields are filled
2. Look for red error messages
3. Fix each error one by one
4. Wait for validation to complete (green checkmark)

**Issue**: "ML optimization is slow"
**Solution**:
- Normal for 500+ spots (takes 3-5 seconds)
- For 1000+ spots, may take up to 10 seconds
- Don't close the window while optimizing

**Issue**: "Can't see my campaigns"
**Solution**:
1. Check filters (might be filtering them out)
2. Click "Clear Filters"
3. Verify you have permission to view campaigns

**Issue**: "Properties panel won't open"
**Solution**:
1. Make sure you've filled basic campaign info first
2. Try clicking the toggle button again
3. Refresh the page if issue persists
